# SkyWave Airlines - Customer Service FAQ

## Baggage
**Q: What is the baggage allowance?**
A: Economy class passengers are allowed 1 carry-on bag (max 10kg) and 1 checked bag (max 23kg). Business class passengers are allowed 2 checked bags (max 32kg each).

**Q: What are the fees for extra baggage?**
A: Extra checked bags cost $60 per bag per flight. Overweight bags (23-32kg) incur a $50 fee.

**Q: What items are prohibited in carry-on baggage?**
A: Liquids over 100ml, sharp objects, flammable items, and lithium batteries over 100Wh are prohibited in carry-on bags.

## Check-In
**Q: When does online check-in open?**
A: Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 2 hours before departure.

**Q: Where can I check in online?**
A: You can check in at skywave.com/checkin or via the SkyWave mobile app.

**Q: What do I need to check in?**
A: You need your booking reference number and passport or government-issued ID.

## Flight Changes & Cancellations
**Q: Can I change my flight?**
A: Yes. Changes are allowed up to 24 hours before departure. A change fee of $75 applies for Economy tickets. Business class tickets can be changed for free.

**Q: Can I cancel my flight and get a refund?**
A: Fully refundable tickets can be cancelled anytime. Non-refundable tickets can be cancelled for a travel credit minus a $50 cancellation fee.

**Q: What happens if SkyWave cancels my flight?**
A: If we cancel your flight, you are entitled to a full refund or free rebooking on the next available flight.

## Seats
**Q: How do I select a seat?**
A: Seats can be selected during booking or through Manage My Booking at skywave.com.

**Q: Is seat selection free?**
A: Standard seats are free for Business class. Economy passengers can select seats for free at check-in or pay $15-$30 in advance for preferred seats.

## Loyalty Program
**Q: How do I join the SkyWave rewards program?**
A: Sign up for free at skywave.com/rewards. You earn 1 point per $1 spent on flights.

**Q: How do I redeem my points?**
A: Points can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, and partner hotels at skywave.com/rewards.

## Special Assistance
**Q: How do I request a wheelchair?**
A: Contact us at least 48 hours before departure at support@skywave.com or call 1-800-SKY-WAVE.

**Q: Can I travel with a pet?**
A: Small pets (under 8kg including carrier) are allowed in the cabin for a $75 fee. Larger pets must travel as checked baggage.
